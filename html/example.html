Default

.pogo is required to make use of PoGO-Awesome.

Sizes

.pogo-xs is 16x16px
.pogo-s is 32x32px
.pogo-m is 64x64px
.pogo-l is 128x128px
.pogo-xl is 256x256px
.pogo-xxl is 512x512px
